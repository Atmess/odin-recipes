Mississippi Chicken Skewers

These Mississippi Chicken Skewers are a delicious and easy-to-make dish that's perfect for summer barbecues or any occasion.

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

    Directions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Toss the chicken chunks with flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the skewers with oil and add the chicken pieces.
  4. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Baste the skewers with the sauce while grilling.
